# APIwiz Technical Writing Assessment

**Client:** APIwiz (Interview Process)
**Role:** Technical Writer Candidate
**Type:** Documentation Skills Assessment

---

## Overview

Completed a practical technical writing assessment for APIwiz as part of the interview process. The assessment evaluated skills across three core documentation disciplines: user guide creation, FAQ writing, and troubleshooting guide development.

---

## Task 1: Docuwiz User Guide

**Brief:** Create a comprehensive user guide manual for Docuwiz.io, following the Microsoft Writing Style Guide with clear, concise, and user-focused language.

### Scope

- Account creation and onboarding flow
- Platform modules: Guides, References, Recipes, Categories, Workspace Settings
- Creating, managing, and publishing content
- User journey format — step-by-step navigation for new users

### Writing Standards Applied

- Active voice and present tense throughout
- Consistent tone, terminology, and formatting
- Proper headings, lists, notes, and callouts
- Industry-standard documentation practices

### Tools Used

Google Docs, Microsoft Writing Style Guide

## Task 2: TaskFlow FAQ Writing

**Brief:** Write a comprehensive FAQ section (minimum 15 entries) for the TaskFlow project management tool, addressing real user complaints and questions from the support team.

### Sample Questions Covered

| # | Question | Answer |
|---|----------|--------|
| 1 | Why can't I log in? | Ensure you're using the correct email and password. If you've forgotten your password, use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset it. If the issue persists, check that your account hasn't been locked due to multiple failed attempts. |
| 2 | How do I assign tasks to others? | Open the task and click the "Assignees" field. Start typing the team member's name and select them from the dropdown. You can assign multiple members to a single task. |
| 3 | How do I change my password? | Go to Workspace Settings > Account > Change Password. Enter your current password, then your new password, and confirm the change. |
| 4 | What is the difference between Free and Paid plans? | The Free plan includes up to 5 projects, 10 members per project, and basic task management features. Paid plans unlock unlimited projects, advanced reporting, integrations, priority support, and more. |
| 5 | How do I delete my account? | Go to Workspace Settings > Account > Delete Account. Note that this action is permanent and will remove all your data, projects, and task history. |
| 6 | Why am I not receiving email notifications? | Check your notification preferences in Settings > Notifications. Ensure the correct email address is verified. Also check your spam folder — some email providers may filter notification emails. |
| 7 | How many people can be added to one project? | The Free plan allows up to 10 members per project. Paid plans support up to 100 members per project, with enterprise plans offering unlimited members. |
| 8 | Does TaskFlow work on mobile? | Yes, TaskFlow is fully responsive and works on any mobile browser. Native iOS and Android apps are also available for download. |
| 9 | How do I export tasks to PDF? | Open the project or task list, click the "Export" button, and select PDF format. You can choose to export individual tasks or the entire project view. |
| 10 | How do I set task priority? | Open the task, locate the "Priority" dropdown, and select Low, Medium, High, or Critical. You can also set priority from the task list view by clicking the priority icon. |
| 11 | How do I create a subtask? | Open the parent task and click "Add Subtask" under the Subtasks section. Subtasks inherit the parent's project, assignees, and due date by default. |
| 12 | How do I archive completed tasks? | Mark a task as complete, then click the "Archive" button in the task menu. Archived tasks are hidden from the main view but can be accessed in the Archive section. |
| 13 | How do I share a project with external members? | Go to Project Settings > Members > Invite External. Enter the guest's email address and select their permission level (Viewer or Commenter). External members receive an invitation email. |
| 14 | How do I change my profile picture? | Click your avatar in the top-right corner, select "Profile Settings," and upload a new image. Supported formats are PNG, JPG, and GIF (max 2 MB). |
| 15 | How do I use filters in the dashboard? | Click the filter icon in the dashboard toolbar. You can filter by status, priority, assignee, due date, and custom tags. Multiple filters can be combined for precise results. |
| 16 | What happens to my data if I cancel my subscription? | Your data remains accessible in read-only mode for 30 days after cancellation. During this period, you can export your data or reactivate your subscription. After 30 days, the account and data are permanently deleted. |
| 17 | How do I restore deleted tasks? | Go to the Trash/Recycle Bin from the project menu. Select the task you want to restore and click "Restore." Tasks are permanently deleted after 30 days in the trash. |
| 18 | Does TaskFlow integrate with Slack or Google Calendar? | Yes, TaskFlow offers native integrations with Slack, Google Calendar, Microsoft Teams, and Zapier. You can enable integrations from Workspace Settings > Integrations. |
| 19 | How do I set task reminders? | Open the task and toggle "Set Reminder" under the Due Date section. Choose when you want to be notified — options include 1 hour, 1 day, or 1 week before the due date. |
| 20 | What is the difference between Admin and Member roles? | Admins have full access to project and workspace settings, including billing, member management, and deletion permissions. Members can create and manage tasks but cannot modify workspace-level settings. |

## Task 3: TaskFlow Troubleshooting Guide

**Brief:** Write a complete troubleshooting guide for TaskFlow, covering 18 documented issues with a quick reference table, note/tip/warning callouts, and a contact support section.

### Quick Reference Table

| # | Issue | Symptom | Solution |
|---|-------|---------|----------|
| 1 | App crashes on large file uploads | App freezes or closes when uploading files over 10 MB | Compress files before uploading. Use the desktop app for large transfers. |
| 2 | Login fails with special characters | Authentication error when password contains special characters | Reset password using alphanumeric characters only. Password rules have been updated in version 2.5. |
| 3 | Dashboard not loading on Internet Explorer | Blank page or layout errors on IE | Switch to a supported browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari). Internet Explorer is no longer supported. |
| 4 | Email notifications in spam | Notification emails consistently land in spam folder | Add noreply@taskflow.com to your address book. Whitelist the domain in your email settings. |
| 5 | Tasks not syncing across devices | Changes made on mobile don't appear on desktop | Pull-to-refresh on mobile. Ensure both devices are on the latest app version. Syncing occurs every 30 seconds. |
| 6 | Dark mode not working on older Android | UI remains in light mode on Android 8 and below | Dark mode requires Android 9 or later. Update your device OS if possible, or use the light theme. |
| 7 | PDF export fails for long descriptions | Export button does nothing when task has extensive description | Split the task into smaller subtasks and export individually. Descriptions over 10,000 characters may cause export failures. |
| 8 | Search fails with combined filters | Empty results when multiple filters are applied simultaneously | Remove filters one at a time to identify conflicts. Some filter combinations are incompatible in the current version. |
| 9 | Attachment limit reached | "Maximum 5 attachments" error | Remove unused attachments before adding new ones. Consider linking to cloud storage for additional files. |
| 10 | Video files not supported | Error when attaching video files | Convert videos to a link format (YouTube, Vimeo, cloud storage share link). Video files are not currently supported as direct attachments. |
| 11 | Slow performance on large projects | App lag when project exceeds 500 tasks | Archive completed tasks to reduce active task count. Use filters to limit the visible task set. Upgrade to a paid plan for priority performance. |
| 12 | Two-factor authentication code missing | SMS or authenticator app does not show the 2FA code | Ensure your phone has network connectivity. Check your authenticator app's time sync. Contact support if the issue persists. |
| 13 | Auto-logout after inactivity | Session expires after 10 minutes of inactivity | This is a security feature for shared devices. To extend your session, check "Keep me signed in" during login. Session timeout can be adjusted in workspace settings (admin only). |
| 14 | Calendar view empty for tasks without due dates | Tasks with no due date do not appear in calendar view | Calendar view only displays tasks with assigned due dates. Set a due date for each task to make it visible on the calendar. |
| 15 | Google Calendar sync fails in certain time zones | Sync errors for users in UTC+5:30 and other non-standard offsets | Verify that your device time zone matches your TaskFlow profile time zone. Manual re-sync may be required after daylight saving time changes. |
| 16 | Subtasks missing from main task view | Subtasks are created but cannot be seen in the main list | Enable "Show Subtasks" from the View Options menu. Subtasks are hidden by default in the main list view to reduce clutter. |
| 17 | Bulk export taking too long | Export progress bar stalls for large projects with 1,000+ tasks | Large exports are processed in batches. For faster results, export by filtered subsets (by status, assignee, or date range). |
| 18 | Notifications not clearing on mobile | Read notifications remain in the notification center on mobile app | Swipe left on the notification to dismiss it. Pull-to-refresh to sync the read status. The web version clears notifications automatically. |

### Best Practices

!!! tip "Prevent Common Issues"
    Keep your app updated to the latest version. Regularly archive completed tasks to maintain performance. Use supported browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari) for the best experience.

!!! warning "Data Safety"
    Always export important data before making significant account changes. Deleted tasks are retained in the trash for 30 days — this is your window for recovery.

!!! note "Version Compatibility"
    TaskFlow version 2.5+ includes fixes for many of the issues listed above. Check your app version in Settings > About and update if available.

### Contact Support

If you experience an issue not covered in this guide, or if the suggested solutions do not resolve your problem, please contact TaskFlow Support:

- **Email:** support@taskflow.com
- **In-App:** Help > Contact Support (includes diagnostic information)
- **Response Time:** Within 24 hours on business days

When contacting support, please include:

1. Your TaskFlow account email
2. Device and browser information
3. Steps to reproduce the issue
4. Any error messages or screenshots
5. Your TaskFlow version number (Settings > About)
