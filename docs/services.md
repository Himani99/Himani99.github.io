## How We Work Together

### Contract Projects

Ideal for specific documentation deliverables with a clear scope and timeline.

1. **Discovery call** — Understand your needs, audience, and goals
2. **Proposal** — I'll outline scope, timeline, and pricing
3. **Execution** — Regular check-ins, drafts, and revisions
4. **Delivery** — Final documentation in your preferred format

### Full-Time Roles

If you need a dedicated technical writer on your team, I'm open to full-time opportunities that align with my expertise.

### Freelance & Retainer

For ongoing documentation needs, I offer monthly retainers that include a set number of hours for documentation work, reviews, and strategy sessions.
